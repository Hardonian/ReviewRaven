# Metrics & Analytics

## Key Metrics

- **Success Rate**: % of URLs that return a verdict other than UNKNOWN.
- **Average Analysis Time**: Target < 3 seconds.
- **Viral Coefficient**: % of users who share their result card.
- **Conversion**: % of "Paste" actions that lead to a "Verdict View".

## Success Thresholds

- **Retention**: 30% of users return within 30 days for their next purchase.
- **Trust Score**: Qualitative feedback on verdict accuracy.

## Failure Signals

- **Spike in UNKNOWN**: Indicates a scraper block or platform update.
- **High Latency**: Indicates bottleneck in proxy layer or analysis engine.

## Iteration Loop

- Monthly review of "Verdict Accuracy" against manual audits.
- Quarterly update of the underlying signal weighting.
