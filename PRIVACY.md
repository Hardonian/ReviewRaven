# PRIVACY.md - ReviewGhost Transparency

## What Data is Used
- **Product URL**: Used to fetch public review data.
- **Public Reviews**: Text, ratings, dates, and reviewer usernames (as provided by the platform).

## What is NOT Stored
- **User Identity**: We don't know who you are.
- **Browsing History**: We only see the URLs you explicitly paste.
- **Personal Data**: We do not sell data to third parties.

## Analytics
We use lightweight, privacy-focused analytics (e.g., Plausible) to track:
- Total products analyzed.
- Verdict distribution (How many AVOID vs BUY).
- Geographic region of the product (for engine optimization).

## No User Tracking
ReviewGhost does not use cookies for tracking. We do not use "retargeting" ads.
